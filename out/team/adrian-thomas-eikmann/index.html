Adrian Thomas-Eikmann

Decades of experience across a huge variety of event types.

DJ and MC

Adrian has over 20 years of DJ experience ranging from festivals, clubs, restaurants, art openings, weddings and corporate events. His DJ career began on a day in 1994. His band mates couldn't meet for a rehearsal and the show needed to go on. His background in percussion made the transition from musician to DJ very easy. Learning the basics was a cinch and over time his skills became more well honed. Adrian takes pride in being able to provide the appropriate atmosphere for any occasion. His diverse event experience ranges from performer to promoter to stagehand to volunteer coordinator.

He is also very comfortable with a microphone and is an accomplished MC/speaker who knows how to enhance the event while keeping the focus on you; the true stars of the show.
